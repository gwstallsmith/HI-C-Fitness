Upper Body

Upper body workouts use the arms, chest, and back muscles.

Safety is the utmost priority in the gym. This is why workouts are listed as beginner or intermediate.

Beginner workouts can be attempted by those new to the gym. They are meant to teach the fundamentals of weight lifting. These workouts are less dangerous as they do not involve putting weight above lifter's head.

Intermediate workouts should be attempted by those with at least some gym experience. They are meant to address more specific muscle groups. These workouts involve some danger, as the weight can go above the head, or other sensitive areas.
