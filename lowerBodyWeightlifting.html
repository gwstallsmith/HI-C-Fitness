Lower Body Weightlifting

By Semi-Fitness Expert Dylan Sarell

The Following Lower body Workouts inolve Weight lifting of some kind.

If you are new to Fitness be cautious when using equipment like dumbbells and barbells.

If you are experienced Fitness Expert you can add more weight to lift in some of the various weightlifting Excerises.

Deadlifts:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Intermediate

Description: Have A barbell in a resting position on the floor and then grab it with your knees and legs bent. Keeping your back straight, straighten out your knees while pulling the weight up. Then stand up straight.

Weighted-Step-Ups:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner/Intermediate

Description: Stand next to an Elevated Platform, grab 2 dumbbells and hold them arm length. Next step on and off the elevated platform, by swinging your arms. You can alternate lifting up your right and left feet.

Single Leg Romanian Deadlift:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Intermediate

Description: With dumbbells in your hands lift one foot back either off the ground or on a elevated platform. Bring your arms to try and reach your toes. Then bring back your foot and repeat.

Back Squats:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Intermediate

Description: Similar to Normal Squats but you hold a Barbell on your back. You crouch down having your back diagonal and legs at ninety degrees then stand back up

Lateral Lunge (Side Lunge):

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Intermediate

Description: Stand up with two dumbbells in each hand at arm's length. Keeping your right leg straight, step over to the left with your left leg and have each dumbbell on each side of your leg. then straighten back out and repeat with the other leg.

Forward Lunge:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner/Intermediate

Description: Stand up straight with two dumbbells in each hand. Take one leg and move it forward keeping the other leg in place and bending both knees to about 90 degrees. Push back to the starting position and repeat the process with the other leg.

Leg Press:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner

Description: A Machine where you sit down with your legs at a 90 degree angle. Then you push on a platform which is being weighed down by a set about of weight.

Barbell Calf Raise:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Intermediate

Description: Have A Barbell Placed behind your Back. Then have your toes push up and stand on your tippy toes. Pause then come back down and repeat.
