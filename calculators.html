Calculators

Below are some calculators to help you on your fitness journey!

It is important to keep track of different statistics so you get the most out of your exercise and nutrition.

Body Mass Index

BMI Calculator:










Result:

Body Mass Index or BMI is your weight divided by height. A high BMI can indicate high body fat and an increased risk of health problems.

Basal Metabolic Rate

BMR Calculator:










    

Result:

Basal Metabolic Rate or BMR estimates the amount of energy expended by your body while at rest. The final result is the amount of calories you should be consuming daily to maintain weight but may alter with a physical job or energetic lifestyle.

Ideal Body Weight

IBW Calculator:






 

Result:

Ideal Body Weight or IBW determines your ideal weight given your height for the lowest risk of health problems. Weight should ideally be kept within 10 pounds of your IBW.
