Lower Body Workouts
by Semi-Fitness Expert Dylan Sarell

Lower body Workouts are those that focus on using your muscles from your legs. These muscles include Quadriceps, Glutes, Hamstrings, Calves and more.

If you are new to Fitness Try to stick with the Beginner workouts! Which could lead to serious injury. Some workouts invlove lifting heavy weights so be careful.

If you are experienced Fitness Expert then you can try any of the Intermediate Workouts.

Deadlifts:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Intermediate

Description: Have A barbell in a resting position on the floor and then grab it with your knees and legs bent. Keeping your back straight, straighten out your knees while pulling the weight up. Then stand up straight.

Weighted-Step-Ups:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner/Intermediate

Description: Stand next to an Elevated Platform, grab 2 dumbbells and hold them arm length. Next step on and off the elevated platform. You can alternate lifting up your right and left feet.

Squats:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner

Description: Continually bending and unbending your knees. Stand up straight then bend your knees to a ninety degree angle like you are sitting on an imaginary chair. The stand straight again.

Elliptical:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner

Description: An exercise machine where you run in place using pedals to do strides. You can change the incline and intensity of the pedals. There is one with arm levers which exercises both your lower and upper body.

Lateral Lunge (Side Lunge):

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Intermediate

Description: Stand up with two dumbbells in each hand at arm's length. Keeping your right leg straight, step over to the left with your left leg and have each dumbbell on each side of your leg. then straighten back out and repeat with the other leg.

Forward Lunge:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner/Intermediate

Description: Stand up straight with two dumbbells in each hand. Take one leg and move it forward keeping the other leg in place and bending both knees to about 90 degrees. Push back to the starting position and repeat the process with the other leg.

Clamshell:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner

Description: Lay on your side with a band tied around both your thighs. Have one arm hold your head up and the other arm to balance you. Keeping your feet together, lift up your thigh to as high as it will go then back down again.

Reverse Lunge:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner/Intermediate

Description: Standing straight, reach your leg back and crouch down to a 90 degree angle for your front leg. Then straighten out again and repeat with the same leg or switch legs.
