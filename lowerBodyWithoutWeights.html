Lower Body Excerises Without Weights

By Semi-Fitness Expert Dylan Sarell

The Following Lower body workouts provide alternatives for people who don't like weightlifting or want a more kind of lower stressed exercise.

If you are new to Fitness Try to stick with the Beginner workouts!

If you are experienced Fitness Expert then adding speed, weights or resistence could make these workouts more challenging.

Treadmill:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner

Description: Treadmill is a Machine that allows you to run in place. A moving convevor is set to various speeds allowing your to run slow or fast.

Stationary Bike:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner

Description: A Bike Machine is a bike which allows you to bike in place. Adjust the seat and bike by spinning the petals with your feet. You can also adjust the resistence.

Squats:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner

Description: Continually bending and unbending your knees. Stand up straight then bend your knees to a ninety degree angle like you are sitting on an imaginary chair. The stand straight again.

Elliptical:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner

Description: An exercise machine where you run in place using pedals to do strides. You can change the incline and intensity of the pedals. There is one with arm levers which exercises both your lower and upper body.

Step-ups:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner

Description: Stand next to an elevated platform and step onto it with one foot. Then bring the other foot up stand tall on the platform. Come back down one foot at a time and repeat.

Glute Bridge:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner

Description: Lay down on the floor with hands to your side and feet flat on the floor. Than raise your thighs up off the ground hold it for a moment then come down.

Clamshell:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner

Description: Lay on your side with a band tied around both your thighs. Have one arm hold your head up and the other arm to balance you. Keeping your feet together, lift up your thigh to as high as it will go then back down again.

Reverse Lunge:

Click this for a how-to-video!

Level: Beginner/Intermediate

Description: Standing straight, reach your leg back and crouch down to a 90 degree angle for your front leg. Then straighten out again and repeat with the same leg or switch legs.
